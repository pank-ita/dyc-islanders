About the 2026 Finals

The 2026 MICSA Championship Finals is put on by the GOCC Gatorz (Great Oaks Country Club). All 14 MICSA clubs compete over the Finals weekend.

The official website has everything your family needs: ticket purchasing, merchandise, general event information, and more. Purchase tickets soon — VIP spots fill quickly.

Bucket-List Golf Raffle — Inverness Club

The GOCC Gatorz Finals team is raffling off an extraordinary golf experience at Inverness Club in Toledo, Ohio (site of multiple U.S. Opens). The prize package includes:

  • Two rounds of golf for six people at Inverness Club
  • Overnight lodging at an on-property home
  • Food, drinks, carts, and/or caddie fees included
  • To be scheduled during the 2027 golf season

Raffle tickets benefit the 2026 MICSA Finals. Visit the Finals website for details.

Cheer On Your Swimmer — Program Ads

DYC Is the Host Club

Hosting the MICSA Championship Finals is a significant undertaking and a tremendous honor for Detroit Yacht Club. It requires coordination across the entire DYC community — from fundraising now through event day in 2027.

One of our first fundraising efforts for the 2027 Finals is selling raffle tickets at the Venetian on June 27th. We need parent + kid pairs for 1-hour time slots.

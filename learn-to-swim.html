About the Program

The 5-week Learn to Swim program is designed to introduce swimmers to the joy of swimming, build early water safety habits, and help children learn beginning swimming skills. Sessions run daily from 11:30am to 12:00pm at the DYC outdoor pool (shallow area) during the morning practice season.

The full session covers all 22 days for $250. Weekly drop-in options are also available. Custom schedules can be arranged directly with Coach Scott. All payments must be made prior to the child participating in lessons.

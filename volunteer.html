Volunteer

Our meets run smoothly because of our amazing parent volunteers — every shift makes a difference.

Volunteer Policy

Volunteer for a timing shift (or one of the other swim meet duties) and receive a free drink ticket. Families who don't volunteer are charged $100 at the end of the season.

Sign Up to Volunteer

Swim Meet Timing

Help with timing races at home meets, tracking finishes, and passing out awards and ribbons. This is the primary volunteer opportunity each season. Sign up early and fill in gaps where you see them.

Sign Up for Meet Timing

Venetian Booth — June 27, 12:30–4:30pm

Help sell raffle tickets at the Venetian to support DYC hosting the 2027 MICSA Finals. We need parent + kid pairs for 1-hour time slots. Note: this does not count toward your volunteer requirement for the season.

Sign Up for Venetian Booth
Other Volunteer Opportunities
Meet MC

Announce the participants for each race and add color commentary. Ryan Sullivan has volunteered for the 2nd and 3rd home meets — still need someone for the June 18th home meet. Sign up via the Swim Meet Timing SignUpGenius.

Meet DJ

Develop a playlist and run the audio equipment during the meet to play music between swim races. Needed for June 18, June 30, and July 14 meets. Sign up via the Swim Meet Timing SignUpGenius.

Floaty Friday Lead

Arrive at practice at 9am to fill team floaties with air and transport them from DYC storage to the pool deck. Stay until 11am to deflate and put them away. Needed June 19, June 26, July 3, and July 10.

Movie Night Organizer

Choose a night for a movie on the pool deck, coordinate with DYC for snacks and projector setup, and work with lifeguards to staff the event. DYC provides and purchases snacks.

End of Season Trophy Coordinator

Work with Marcia at Connoisseur Apparel to coordinate ordering of end-of-season trophies.
